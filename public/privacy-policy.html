ChroMarx Privacy Policy

Data Collection and Usage

ChroMarx collects and processes the following data:

  • Bookmark data (titles, URLs, and organization)
  • Extension settings and preferences
  • User account information (if you choose to sign in)
  • Anonymous usage statistics (optional and can be disabled)

Data Storage

Your data is stored:

  • Locally on your device using Chrome's storage API
  • In your Chrome sync account (if sync is enabled)
  • No data is sold to third parties

Permissions

ChroMarx requires these permissions:

  • Storage: To save your bookmarks and preferences
  • Bookmarks: To manage your Chrome bookmarks
  • Identity: For optional Google sign-in
  • Tabs: To access current tab information for bookmarking

Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Google Identity Services: For user authentication
  • Google Cloud Services: For AI-powered features (optional)
  • PayPal: For processing payments (optional)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of data collection

Data Security

We protect your data using:

  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Local storage encryption
  • Regular security updates
