ChroMarx Privacy Policy

Data Collection

ChroMarx collects and processes the following data:

  • Bookmark data (titles, URLs, and organization)
  • Extension settings and preferences
  • Anonymous usage statistics (optional)

Data Usage

The collected data is used for:

  • Providing bookmark management functionality
  • Improving the extension's features and performance
  • Personalizing your experience

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. If you choose to enable sync, data will be stored in your Chrome sync account.

Third-Party Services

ChroMarx uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (optional) for user accounts
  • Google Cloud Services for AI-powered features (optional)

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your data
  • Opt-out of data collection
  • Export your data

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us at privacy@chromarx.com

Updates

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Last updated: March 2024
