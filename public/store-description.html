Chrome Web Store Description

Short Description (132 characters max)

Gemini-powered bookmark manager – organize, search, and analyze your bookmarks with AI assistance and offline support.

Detailed Description

ChroMarx transforms how you manage and interact with your bookmarks. Powered by Google's Gemini AI, this intelligent bookmark manager helps you organize, optimize, and extract maximum value from your saved web pages.

Whether you're a researcher, student, professional, or just someone with too many bookmarks, ChroMarx provides the tools to turn your bookmark chaos into a well-organized, searchable knowledge base that works even when you're offline.

Key Features:

  • AI-Powered Organization: Automatically categorize and tag bookmarks based on content
  • Smart Search: Find bookmarks using natural language questions
  • Content Summaries: Get AI-generated summaries of your bookmarked pages
  • Collections: Group related bookmarks into custom collections
  • Offline Access: View and manage your bookmarks even when you're not connected
  • Cross-Device Sync: Access your bookmarks across all your devices
  • Privacy-Focused: Your data stays private with optional encrypted cloud storage
  • Productivity Tools: Task extraction, notes, timers, and more

Who ChroMarx is for:

  • Researchers tracking multiple sources
  • Students organizing study materials
  • Professionals managing work resources
  • Anyone overwhelmed by bookmark overload

ChroMarx respects your privacy - all AI processing is done securely, and you control what data is stored and shared.

Upgrade your browsing experience with ChroMarx – where AI meets organization to make your online life more productive.
